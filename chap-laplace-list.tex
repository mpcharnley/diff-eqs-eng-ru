Heaviside function, $\delta$ is the Dirac delta function, and 
\begin{equation*}
\Gamma(t) =
\int_0^\infty e^{-\tau} \tau^{t-1} \, d\tau ,
\qquad
\operatorname{erf}(t) =
\frac{2}{\sqrt{\pi}} \int_0^t e^{-\tau^2} \, d\tau ,
\qquad
\operatorname{erfc}(t) =
%\frac{2}{\sqrt{\pi}} \int_t^\infty e^{-\tau^2} \, d\tau =
1 - \operatorname{erf}(t) .
\end{equation*}
